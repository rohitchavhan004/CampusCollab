About US

SCOE STUDENTS

Are you looking for exciting events and activities happening in and around our sinhgad college campus? Look no further! CampusCollab. Com is you go to website for all things related to college events, activities. We provide a platform where students can showcase what they have got, which helps to discover their talents, interests apart from academics. From sports tournament to music festivals, cultural celebrations to academic competitions, we have it all covered. Our website features a user friendly interface that allows you to easily search and browse through a wide range of events that clubs arrange across the campus of 'Sinhgad institute of technical education society'. Our websites main feature is students can register in different clubs online and contact the in charge as per their need of interest. Motive of the website is to provide a simple registration platform so that students can register to their particular interest based club easily and stay updated.
